About Us

At BytesLogik LLC, we specialize in analyzing the security of post-quantum cryptographic algorithms. Our mission is to stay at the forefront of cryptographic security by investigating potential attack vectors and developing quantum algorithms designed to challenge and evaluate the resilience of these next-generation cryptographic solutions.

Our research focuses on identifying weaknesses in PQC protocols and running simulations of quantum attacks to anticipate and mitigate potential threats in the evolving quantum computing landscape.

Our Services

  • Quantum Attack Simulation: Developing and testing quantum-based attack algorithms against post-quantum cryptographic schemes to assess their vulnerability.
  • Security Analysis of PQC Algorithms: Analyzing the security of lattice-based, code-based, hash-based, and multivariate polynomial cryptographic algorithms.
  • Vulnerability Assessment: Evaluating potential weaknesses in PQC standards and providing risk assessments for specific attack vectors.
  • Research & Development in Quantum Algorithm Design: Creating and optimizing quantum algorithms that model potential real-world attacks on PQC schemes.
